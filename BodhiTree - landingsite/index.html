About Me


Julie Taft is a licensed mental health counselor with twenty years of experience working in school, agency, and private practice settings. She has extensive experience working with diverse populations of children, adolescents, and adults. She is trained in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing), an empirically supported treatment for victims of trauma. In the past, she has also worked with substance abuse clients. She has a client-centered approach to counseling and therapy, allowing the client to take an active role in the treatment.
